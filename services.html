Advanced Infrastructure

We plan, design, build, maintain and monitor data networks and telecommunications infrastructure for telcos, governments and corporates. Our infrastructure services include the installation and maintenance of the network, wireless infrastructure, and the security thereof.

Software Services

We help organisations increase productivity and efficiency through automating business processes, and simplifying data processing and workflows. Dv8's software development team's key objective is enhancing the effectiveness of customers' systems and processes and thus delivering increased profitability. To this end, we offer bespoke software solutions tailored for the specific needs of individual clients.

Cyber Security

A data breach can mean financial and reputational damage for an organisation of any size. In an increasingly complex and connectivity-dependent world, we provide products, services and solutions that guarantee the security and accessibility of your network and data when you need it. We also provide security awareness campaigns, training and workshops for clients’ staff.

Managed Services

Let our team of experts take charge of your entire organisations’ IT needs, leaving you free to focus on your core business and delivering value to your clients. Our team of highly-skilled professionals will ensure that your organisations’ network is fully operational and that your business applications and systems are optimised and are up and running. Our team of experts will proactively manage all your IT requirements 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Business Consulting

As experts in the IT field, we can assist organisations design and implement blueprints on how best to use technology to enhance performance. Our business consulting services help position our clients to be able to respond rapidly to the ever-changing business landscape through state-of-the-art ICT solutions. These range from the business intelligence gathering, productivity, and performance monitoring and evaluation innovations – all geared towards boosting capacity to achieve business objectives.

IT Products & Services

DV8 provides a wide range of hardware and software products and services. These include bundled solutions for specific business and organisational needs. The product and service offerings comprise accounting, security, cloud storage, business intelligence, productivity and efficiency solutions.
